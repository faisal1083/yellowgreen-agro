Who We Are

Yellow Green Agro Ltd. is a modern, integrated agribusiness. We combine dairy, poultry & ducks, aquaculture, vegetables, seeds & nursery with small-batch processing of ghee, pickles, spice blends, oils, and sweets.

Our promise is simple: grow clean, process carefully, and deliver honestly. That means full traceability, better animal care, and residue-aware cultivation from soil to shelf.

  • Animal welfare & hygiene protocols
  • Residue-aware cultivation & water quality control
  • Export documentation & packaging readiness
  • Sustainable organic manure & soil health

Our Story

Roots in Responsible Farming

We started as a small family farm with a big idea — produce food we’d proudly feed our own families. Over time, we added nursery, fish ponds, and ethical poultry & dairy units.

Integrated, Not Isolated

Our farm cycles manure into soil nourishment, uses water responsibly, and adopts multi-species planning to keep the ecosystem balanced and resilient.

From Local to Global

With export-ready packaging and documentation, we serve both local households and B2B buyers — delivering consistent quality and traceability throughout the chain.

What We Produce

Food Safety & Quality

Our handling, chilling, and packaging SOPs are designed to protect freshness and reduce contamination risks. Each batch is recorded for traceability.

  • Sanitized milking and cold chain for dairy
  • Dedicated processing for cold-pressed oils
  • Clean cutting, icing, and transport for fish
  • Lot coding & labeling for recall readiness
