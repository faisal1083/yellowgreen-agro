About Yellow Green Agro

We operate a modern integrated farm combining dairy, poultry, ducks, aquaculture, and vegetables with seed nurseries and processing facilities. Our goal is to bring safe, traceable, and honest food from farm to table — grown responsibly in Bangladesh.

Through innovation, soil health management, and ethical animal care, we ensure sustainability at every stage — from feeding our animals to packaging our products.

  • Animal welfare & hygiene protocols
  • Residue-free cultivation & water quality control
  • Export documentation & packaging readiness
  • Sustainable organic manure & soil health
